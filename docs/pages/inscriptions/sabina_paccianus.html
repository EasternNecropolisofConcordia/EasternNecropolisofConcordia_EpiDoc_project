Palaeographic Comment

Writing direction left to right; horizontal alignment; the first two lines are the most carefully executed: square module, with the presence of thick and thin strokes creating a chiaroscuro effect, while in the subsequent lines, the incision is shallower and the letters display a more laterally compressed module; regular ductus, centred layout.

INSCRIPTION

TRANSCRIPTION

DD(is) MM(anibus) Cusica Sabina sibi et Ennio Pac = ciano, coniugi dulcissimo, vi = va posuit

To the Manes gods.

Cusica Sabina, while still alive, arranged (the sarcophagus) for herself and for Ennius Paccianus, her most affectionate husband.

APPARATUS CRITICUS

5. DVLCI[S]SIM[O], Bertolini 1877 .
