The Epigraphy of Alexander

Findspot

City:Concordia Sagittaria

Ancient city: Iulia Concordia

Date: First Half of the Fourth Century.

INSCRIPTION

TRANSCRIPTION

Alexandro filio piissimo mirae verecundiae, singulari<s> innocentiae, castitat<a>e, integ = ro notarum litteris erudito, qui vixit ạnn(is) XVII. Sabbatia mater ad ultimum vitae deflens exitum fili et sibi fecit.

To Alexander, most devoted son, of remarkable modesty, singular innocence, chastity, upright, learned in shorthand signs, who lived 17 years.

(His) mother Sabbatia, mourning the end of her son's life, made (this monument) also for herself.
