INSCRIPTION

TRANSCRIPTION

Fl(avio) Maximiano fideli, filio caṛissimo, Fl(avius) Maximianus fidelis pater, arcam de pro = prio s(uo) vivus con = paravit (!) et sibi{s}.

To Flavius Maximianus, a loyal and most beloved son, his faithful father Flavius Maximianus made this chest from his own means while still alive, and for himself.
