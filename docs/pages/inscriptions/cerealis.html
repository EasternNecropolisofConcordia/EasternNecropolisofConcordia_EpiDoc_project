PART b:

Ennia Asteria, Enni Asteri filia, ex origine Enni Cerealis avi sui arcam suo nomine renovavit, quam post ex = cessum suum si quis inruperit (!) inferet fisci rationibus HS (!) CC NN(ummum). Fili sane veniam habebunt.

Ennia Asteria, daughter of Ennius Asterius, restored in her own name the arca that had belonged to her grandfather Ennius Cerealis.

If anyone should violate it after her death, he shall pay two hundred sesterces to the accounts of the fiscus. Her children, however, shall be exempt.
