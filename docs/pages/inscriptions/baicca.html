Ego magister Baicca, de proprio meo arca<m> mihi conparavi (!). Si qui eam voluerit aperire, davit (!) fisci viribus arg(enti) p(ondo) n(ummum) V.

I, Baicca, master, purchased this coffin for myself with my own means. Whoever opens it shall pay to the fisc five pounds of silver.
