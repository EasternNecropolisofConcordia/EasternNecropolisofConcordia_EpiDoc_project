TRANSCRIPTION

F{a}l(avius) Costantius, pientiss = mo filio Eutherio, amis<s>ion = e et dolore orbatus, memo = riam corpori eius posui, cui = us asum aliquid sol = acii fore confido, vixit annos XXVIII.

Flavius Costantius, to his most devoted son Eutherius, bereft by loss and grief, have set up this memorial (sarcophagus) to his body, in the sight of which I trust there will be some solace. He lived twenty-eight years.

APPARATUS CRITICUS

2-3. AMISSIONE, Lettich 1983 .
