The Epigraphy of Ursus and Decentia

Findspot

City:Concordia Sagittaria

Ancient city: Iulia Concordia

Date: First Half of the Fourth Century A.D.

INSCRIPTION

TRANSCRIPTION

Cocceius Ursus conparavit (!) arca (!) uxori sụạe inconparavili (!) Decentiae, qụe (!) vixit me{ce}cu<m> annis XI, men(sibus) X, die<bu>s XXV, ut et ego post ovitu<m> (!) meu<m> con (!) ipsa ponar.

Cocceius Ursus purchased the arca for his peerless wife Decentia, who lived with me for 11 years, 10 months, and 25 days, so that I too, after my death, may be laid to rest with her.
